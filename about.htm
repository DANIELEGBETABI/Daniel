Just some short info about me, I was born in Sweden and later moved to Oslo, Norway in 1999. I studied math, physics, computer science and astronomy at Uppsla University in Sweden and University of Oslo in Norway. I have a Candidatus Scientiarum degree in Mathematical modelling.

I have been working for Opera Software in Oslo for a number of years and I love it.

I am happily married to my beautiful wife Anastasia and in September 2011 we got our firstborn Franz Bertil. If I am not working I like to spend some time photographing or taking a ride on my bike in the Norwegian nature. Playing some games is never bad either, but too bad fun games takes so much time.
